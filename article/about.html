About the conference

Held as a part of Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conferences (UGM-ASC 2017) series, the 2nd International Conference on Tropical Agriculture (ICTA 2017) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe agriculture, forestry, and animal production issues and socio-economics aspect of it as well.

This year, five symposia will be held, Sustainable tropical forestry, Innovative and emerging agricultural technology and management, Sustainable Tropical Agriculture, Sustainable tropical animal production and health, and Socio-economic dimension in tropical agriculture.

The selected papers will be published in journals/proceedings that are indexed by Scopus or accredited by DIKTI: Springer Proceeding*, Agritech*, Jurnal Sain Veteriner*, Jurnal Kehutanan*, PPAS*, Buletin Peternakan*, Indonesian Journal of Biotechnology*, Jurnal Keteknikan Pertanian*.
